DMV Permit Practice Pro is a study app for California and New York DMV permit written test preparation. This page explains what information the app may use, how that information may be handled, and how to contact us with privacy questions.

What this app does

DMV Permit Practice Pro helps users prepare for California and New York DMV permit written tests with multilingual practice, road sign drills, mock tests, handbook access, review tools, exam guides, premium study features, and supported content updates. The app currently supports English, Simplified Chinese, Spanish, Vietnamese, Tagalog / Filipino, Haitian Creole, and Korean study help.

What information may be used

  • Learning activity such as answer history, saved questions, mistakes, mock test results, handbook progress, road sign activity, and local study preferences
  • Device or app usage information needed for app stability, diagnostics, troubleshooting, or content delivery
  • Advertising-related information when ads are shown inside the free version of the app
  • Purchase or subscription status when premium features are offered

How the information is used

  • To save study progress and improve review features
  • To support practice sessions, mock tests, road sign drills, handbook study, and exam guides
  • To refresh supported DMV study content when remote updates are enabled
  • To understand app performance and fix bugs
  • To display ads in the free version of the app
  • To manage premium access and restore purchases

Local storage

Study data such as attempts, saved questions, mistakes, mock test results, handbook progress, road sign activity, and preferences are primarily stored on the device.

Third-party services

The app may use third-party services for advertising, consent collection, purchases, diagnostics, analytics, or content delivery. Those services may process device identifiers, purchase status, ad interaction data, or technical usage data under their own policies.

Advertising and tracking

The free version of the app may display ads. Depending on your region and device settings, the app may show consent prompts related to advertising, tracking, or privacy choices, including iOS App Tracking Transparency prompts where applicable.

Children's privacy

This app is designed as a general educational and test-preparation tool. It is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

You can choose whether to continue using the app, whether to purchase premium features, and how to respond to privacy or advertising consent prompts shown by your device or region.
