PRIVACY POLICY

DMV Permit Pro / DMV笔试通

Last updated: April 3, 2026

DMV Permit Pro is a study app for California and New York DMV permit preparation. This page explains what information the app may use, how that information may be handled, what third-party services may be involved, and how to contact us with privacy questions.

ENGLISH

What this app does

DMV Permit Pro helps users prepare for California and New York DMV permit written tests with bilingual practice, road sign drills, mock tests, handbook access, review tools, and exam guides.

What information may be used

  • Learning activity such as answer history, saved questions, mistakes, and mock test results
  • Device or app usage information needed for app stability, analytics, and troubleshooting
  • Advertising-related information when ads are shown inside the app

Third-party services

The app may use third-party services for advertising, consent collection, crash diagnostics, or analytics. Those services may process device identifiers, technical usage data, or ad interaction data in accordance with their own policies.

How the information is used

  • To save your learning progress and improve review features
  • To understand app performance and fix bugs
  • To display and measure advertisements

Local storage

Your study data, such as question attempts, mistakes, saved items, and mock test results, is primarily stored on your device.

Advertising and analytics

The app may use advertising and analytics services that process device identifiers or contextual data for ad delivery, measurement, and product improvement.

Your choices

You may choose whether to continue using the app. Depending on your region and device settings, you may also see consent prompts related to tracking, advertising, or privacy choices on iOS.

Children's privacy

This app is designed as a general educational and test-preparation tool. It is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: dmvpermitpro@gmail.com
