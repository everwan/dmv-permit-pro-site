ENGLISH

Common support topics

  • Question or explanation issues
  • App crashes or display bugs
  • Ad display problems
  • Language display issues
  • Handbook tab, chapter progress, or review flow issues
  • Feature requests and product feedback

What to include

  • Your device model
  • Your iOS version
  • The screen where the problem happened
  • A screenshot if available
  • Your app language and whether the issue happened in practice, mock test, handbook, review, or guide

Typical response scope

Support is mainly provided for content issues, app bugs, ad display problems, and general usage questions. We may not be able to provide legal, licensing, or DMV policy advice beyond the study materials included in the app.
